**Voice Register Summary**
Professional but warm, direct, concise, no fluff, relationship-aware. 
- Use first names and acknowledge specific context.
- Get straight to the ask or the point.
- Use clean formatting with clear paragraph breaks.
- Avoid corporate jargon where plain English works better.
- When writing long-form content (like Substack or LinkedIn), adopt a reflective, narrative-driven tone that connects personal insights with broader industry trends (e.g., the shift from PM to founder, the "second half" of a career).
