I run two distinct businesses:

**1. DMPtech.ai (B2B SaaS / Enterprise)**
- **What we do**: We provide an AI-native regulatory, supervisory, and governance platform. We transform static compliance manuals into a living, breathing governance operating system.
- **Target Audience**: Financial Services (SEC-governed, FINRA-member broker-dealers), Wealth Management & RIAs, and FinTech companies.
- **Core Value**: Replacing legacy platforms with an active, self-evolving governance layer that automates compliance workflows and provides a real-time Compliance Alignment Score.

**2. Easein (B2C E-commerce / Sustainable Apparel)**
- **What we do**: We make the world's first circular performance socks. They are 100% plant-based (PLA fiber), dermatologist-tested, NFC-tracked for sustainability, and designed to be returned and recycled into new pairs.
- **Target Audience**: Environmentally conscious consumers, athletes, professionals, and people with sensitive skin who value performance and sustainability.
- **Core Value**: Closing the loop on apparel waste. Customers wear them, return them (scanning an NFC tag for a free label), and we renew the materials while crediting their account. Currently in pre-order phase, shipping Summer 2026.
